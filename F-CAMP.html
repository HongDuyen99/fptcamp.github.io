Introduce

As usual, the F-Camp event becomes a banquet exclusively for new recruits joining FPTU's roof. On November 8, F-Camp 2020 took place throughout the day and marked countless special moments with hundreds of K16 students FPTU Hanoi.
It can be said that F-Camp is not just a festival, but also a continuation of the culture and the giving of values ​​that have formed the "soul" of FPT University and FPT students. With this special gift, the Organizing Committee wants the new students to always burn their hearts out with their dreams, dare to commit themselves to aspirations and passions and together mark their own youth with brilliant colors.
